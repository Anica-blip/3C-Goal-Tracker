About Dash Notes

A private thinking space for fast minds.

Built around the 3C Goal Setting Framework and the Eisenhower Decision Matrix — this app helps you set meaningful goals, sort priorities, and capture thoughts before they disappear.

Works best for people who

Think faster than they can organise · Want goals that reflect real values · Need to capture ideas on the move · Prefer clarity over noise

Privacy

No server. No account. No data leaves your device. Everything is stored privately in your browser. Use the Save section to back up your data regularly.

Best Practice

For notes you want to keep long term, download a backup regularly and keep updating that same note over time. The JSON file becomes your running plan — always recoverable. Use Store for shorter notes that get acted on and discarded. Both work together.

🔒 Important: Clearing your browser's site data will permanently delete your Dash Notes data. Always share or download a backup first. Your data. Your responsibility. Your freedom.
