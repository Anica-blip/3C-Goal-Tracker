What This App Is

3C Goal Tracker is a private, device-based tool designed to help you turn thoughts into direction.

Built around the 3C Goal Setting Framework and the Eisenhower Decision Matrix, it helps you set meaningful goals, organise priorities, and reflect with clarity — instead of overwhelm.

This is not a productivity app. It is a clarity app.
Designed for people who think fast, capture ideas in motion, and need a structured space to organise what matters before it gets lost.

Privacy by Design
🔒 No server. No database. No account.
Everything is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB — private to your device and inaccessible to anyone else, including the app developers.
🎙️ Voice recordings are converted into text using your browser's built-in speech recognition. The audio itself is never stored.
📷 Images captured with your camera remain on your device and are not included in backups.
💾 Download a JSON backup or email your data to yourself anytime. Your data. Your responsibility. Your freedom.
Keeping Your Data Safe — Simple Steps

Since your data lives on your device, here is how to protect it. No tech knowledge needed.

1
Use the app regularly on the same browser.
Your data is stored in that browser. Switching browsers or devices means starting fresh.
2
Email yourself a backup after each important session.
One tap in the app — your goals and notes arrive in your inbox as readable text, ready to restore.
3
Do not clear your browser storage without backing up first.
Clearing site data, cookies, or cache will delete your Goal Tracker data.
4
Download a JSON file as a second backup.
Save it to your phone, your computer, or your cloud storage. It is a complete snapshot of your data.
💡 The goal tracker works best when used consistently on the same device. Think of it like a private journal that lives in your pocket — organised, protected, and yours.
